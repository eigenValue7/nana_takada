Interactive Device Design

MeowPow

A soothing smart plushie that provides comforting light, sound, and haptic feedback to support emotional regulation. Rather than relying on verbal instruction, MewoPow responds to hugs with gentle light, vibration, and sound, inviting sustained embraces into a slow breathing rhythm using the 4-7-8 pattern.

For Individuals seeking emotional regulation support

AI for Parenting Support

PCIT Coaching System

An AI-powered coaching system that delivers personalized, context-grounded feedback by analyzing what parents actually say during interactions with their children. Combines speaker diarization, automatic speech recognition, and a fine-tuned RoBERTa classifier (83.6% accuracy) to identify and categorize parent utterances.

For Parents in PCIT therapy, clinicians
Tech
RoBERTa LangGraph Whisper GPT-4
IRB study in preparation

Mental Health Technology

New Beginnings Program Chatbot

A chatbot intervention for divorced parents based on the New Beginnings Program, an evidence-based intervention that teaches parents skills to prevent children from becoming caught in interparental conflict. The system prompts users to reflect on recent child-involved conflicts and guides them to rewrite their behaviors.

For Divorced parents with children
Tech
Streamlit LangChain OpenAI API
Feasibility interviews with psychiatrists

Human-Robot Interaction

RoboSketch

A collaborative drawing robot game that translates children's movement into visual traces. As a child moves through space, the robot follows and draws their path on paper, revealing how robots can mediate human-to-human connection through play. Tested with K-12 students.

For K-12 students, educational settings

Interactive Device Design

ColorCatcher

A handheld drawing board that lets users "catch" real-world colors from their environment. Point the device at any object—a flower, a wall, a sunset—and paint with that exact color. Combines a color sensor, joystick for drawing, rotary encoder for brush size, and capacitive touch buttons for tool switching.

For Creative expression, accessibility

Psychology of Social Computing

Breeze or Bolt

A study investigating how aligning a chatbot's language intensity with a user's impacts perceptions of trustworthiness, persuasiveness, and relational appeal. Created high and low-intensity chatbots using chain-of-thought prompting to explore the similarity effect in human-AI interaction.

For Understanding chatbot design for trust
